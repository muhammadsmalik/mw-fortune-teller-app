# MW Product Mapping

## Media Owner

### How can I sell my inventory faster or directly?

Recommended path: MW Studio

Meaning: Build your brand's direct gateway to OOH visibility and presence.

Product recommendation: Measurement & Reporting

Value: Offer advertisers proof of performance and premium insights as a value-added service.

Features:

- Brand Lift Studies
- Automated Campaign Reports
- Campaign Insights

### How can I get SMEs and brands to notice my screens?

Recommended path: MW Market

Meaning: Sell directly to advertisers and SMEs with marketplace access.

Product recommendation: Campaign Intelligence

Value: Arm your sales team with pitch decks and reports built on real audience and delivery data.

Features:

- Automated Pitch Decks
- Case Study Builder
- Audience Data

### What's the best way to automate campaign scheduling?

Recommended path: MW Planner

Meaning: Automate scheduling and optimise inventory management.

Product recommendation: LMX Platform

Value: Centralize inventory, standardize media packs, and enable programmatic access to global demand.

Features:

- Inventory Management
- Programmatic Access
- API Integrations

### How can I benchmark my screen performance against others?

Recommended path: MW Measure

Meaning: Benchmark and prove your screen performance with data.

Product recommendation: Self-Serve Booking Platform

Value: Monetize long-tail advertisers by allowing them to book and manage campaigns directly.

Features:

- No-Code Tools
- Direct Booking Portal
- Billing & Invoicing

### How do I prepare for programmatic OOH?

Recommended path: MW Activate

Meaning: Prepare for programmatic with automated campaign delivery.

Product recommendation: Audience Intelligence

Value: Use location and movement data to build tailored audience profiles for every screen.

Features:

- Audience Profiling
- Geo-Targeting
- Planner Tool

### How can AI help me predict demand or pricing?

Recommended path: MW Science

Meaning: Use AI to predict demand, optimise pricing, and forecast revenue.

Product recommendation: Self-Serve Booking Platform

Value: Enable smaller advertisers to run quick test campaigns with minimal friction via packages and deals.

Features:

- Packaged Deals
- Instant Booking
- Marketplace Showcase
